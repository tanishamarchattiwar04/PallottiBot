Who We Are
A Legacy of Engineering
& Compassionate Education
Founded by the Diocese of Nagpur, SVPCET combines technical excellence with the values of Saint Vincent Pallotti — service, knowledge, and character.
🏛️
📍 Gavsi Manapur, Wardha Road, Nagpur

Established in 2008, SVPCET is one of Nagpur's premier engineering institutions, affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU) and approved by AICTE. Our campus fosters an environment of academic inquiry, technical innovation, and value-based education.

Admissions 2026-27
Secure your seat through MHT-CET / JEE Main. The admission process is streamlined and transparent through DTE Maharashtra's CAP rounds.
1

Appear for Entrance

MHT-CET or JEE Main for engineering. MAH-MBA CET for MBA.

2

Register on DTE Portal

Submit scores and preferences on dtemaharashtra.gov.in

3

CAP Round Allotment

Receive your college and branch allotment based on merit and preference.

4

Report to SVPCET

Submit documents, pay fees, and confirm your admission.

📋 Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Marksheets
  • MHT-CET / JEE Scorecard
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate (Maharashtra)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport-size Photographs

💰 Fee Structure (Approx.)

  • B.E. / B.Tech: ₹1,00,000–₹1,30,000/year
  • MBA: ₹80,000–₹1,00,000/year
  • SC/ST/OBC fee waiver via government schemes
  • EWS category benefits available
  • Scholarships for meritorious students
  • Direct 2nd Year (Diploma Lateral Entry)
  • Hostel & transport fees separate
